Chicken Breast

Description

Air frying is the best way to cook tender chicken in less time,

since there's no preheating the oven. And with my simple seasoning blend,

flavorful chicken breasts not only make an easy weeknight dinner,

but they're perfect for meal prep too. This recipe results in super flavorful

,tender chicken breasts with no brining!

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast 200-400gr.
  • Salt, pepper
  • Lemon

Steps

  1. Cut chicken on cubes
  2. Mix lemon and spices with the chicken
  3. Put chicken on 180Â°C for 6 minutes in air frier
  4. After 6 minutes mix the chicken and put it for 6 more minutes in the same temperature
