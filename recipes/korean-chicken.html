Korean Friend Spicy Chicken

Description

The best Korean Fried Chicken - crispy coated buttermilk fried chicken smothered in a spicy Korean-inspired gochujang sauce.

The sauce is so rich, flavourful and complex and it comes together very easily - just throw all the ingredients in a pan and bubble it up.

We love to eat this with rice, noodles, or my personal favourite - stuffed into bao buns!

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast 700gr.
  • Cornflour 25gr.
  • Salt, Paprika, Pepper, Powdered Garlic
  • Gochujang 40gr.
  • Honey 25gr.
  • Soy sauce 50ml.
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Cut chicken to cubes
  2. Mix cornflour and spices until coated
  3. Fry on high-medium temparuter until golden brown
  4. On pan mix soy sauce, gochujang, honey until sticky
  5. Put chicken in the sticky sauce and cook until coated
