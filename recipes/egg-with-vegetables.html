Eggs with vegetables

Description

Indulge in a delightful breakfast experience with our Savory Scrambled Eggs featuring the irresistible trio of red peppers, mushrooms, and onions.

This recipe combines the richness of saut√©ed mushrooms, the sweetness of caramelized onions,

and the vibrant crunch of red peppers with fluffy scrambled eggs, delivering a burst of flavors and textures to kickstart your day.

Easy to make and endlessly customizable, it's the perfect way to elevate your morning routine. Join us in the kitchen and savor the deliciousness!

Ingredients

  • 5 Eggs
  • 2 Red peppers
  • 1-2 Onions
  • 4-5 Mushrooms
  • Butter 10 gr.
  1. Cut the veges
  2. Heat the pan to medium-high and put half of the butter
  3. Cook the veges until the desired brown
  4. Crack the eggs in the pan with the veges with the other half of the butter
  5. Cook until the eggs are cooked and serve!
